## Part 6: Building Strategic Relationships

### Approach Strategy
1. **Research**: Understand partner's goals/needs
2. **Personalize**: Custom outreach (no mass emails)
3. **Value First**: Show how partnership benefits them
4. **Data**: Use metrics to prove value
5. **Follow Up**: 2-3 follow-ups over 2 weeks
6. **Meeting**: Always request a call/meeting
7. **Agreement**: Formal contract with clear terms

### Relationship Management
- **Quarterly Reviews**: Check-in on performance
- **Monthly Updates**: Share metrics and news
- **Rapid Response**: Quick issue resolution
- **Dedicated Support**: Account manager for major partners
- **Co-Marketing**: Joint promotional activities
- **Revenue Share**: Transparent, timely payments
