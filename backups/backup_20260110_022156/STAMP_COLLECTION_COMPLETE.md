- Only 100 sheets were sold
- Mint condition example

### Penny Black (Great Britain, 1840)
**Value:** $625 | 6,250 STMP
- World's first adhesive postage stamp
- Portrait of Queen Victoria
- Revolutionary postal innovation
- Foundation of modern mail system

### Tre Skilling Bank (Sweden, 1855)
**Value:** $1,560 | 15,600 STMP

- One of rarest stamps in world
- Swedish postal history
