## Artist Statement
We digitize and preserve philatelic history through high-fidelity NFTs representing legendary stamps. Each piece is backed by expert verification, historical context, and recorded provenance.

## Concept & Aesthetics
Each NFT combines archival imagery, expert-authenticated metadata, and refined presentation. The works emphasize rarity, history, and cultural value — a bridge between traditional collecting and web3.

## Technical Details
- ERC-721 NFTs on Polygon
- IPFS metadata & media
- tRPC-based backend for verification and minting
- Drizzle ORM-backed provenance tables
