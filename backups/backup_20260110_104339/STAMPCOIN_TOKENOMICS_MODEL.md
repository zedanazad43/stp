### 2.2 Pricing Factors

**Base Price = (Year Age + Rarity Multiplier + Catalog Value + Condition Grade)**

- **Year Age Factor:** Older stamps worth more (+1% per 10 years)
- **Rarity Multiplier:** Common (1x), Uncommon (3x), Rare (8x), Very Rare (25x), Legendary (100x+)
- **Catalog Value:** Scott's/Stanley Gibbons pricing (30% premium for verified catalog items)
- **Condition Grade:** Mint (-10%), Fine (-5%), Very Fine (baseline), Used (+5%), Poor (+10%)
